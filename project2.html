INDIA TOURISM

One of the oldest civilisations in the world, India is a mosaic of multicultural experiences. With a rich
heritage and myriad attractions, the country is among the most popular tourist destinations in the world.
It covers an area of 32, 87,263 sq. km, extending from the snow-covered Himalayan heights to the
tropical rain forests of the south. As the 7th largest country in the world, India stands apart from the rest
of Asia, marked off as it is by mountains and the sea, which give the country a distinct geographical entity.
